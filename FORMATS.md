# Formats, Franchises, Funnel, Campaign Tracker

A creator is built on recurring formats and an arc, not scattershot one-offs. Every clip routes INTO a series, and the day's posts ladder up to July 30. This is the Kim K / Sevyn add: serialized storylines + attention-to-commerce.

## Recurring series registry (tag every clip to one)
Maintain a running registry. Starter set for a catering founder + lifestyle creator:
- **30 Days to Plated** · countdown series, one beat per day to the event. Numbered episodes.
- **Founder Diaries** · building a catering brand from nothing, the real mess and wins.
- **A Day in the Life of a Founder** · Fan Fest, mic-buying, errands, networking ARE episodes of this.
- **Plate It With Me** · POV food prep/styling, sensory, premium craft.
- **Get Ready to Host** · GRWM energy aimed at the future Plated guest.
- **Things Nobody Tells You About Catering** · contrarian/educational, saves + shares.
- **Tasting Notes** · recurring food close-up format, short and beautiful.
- {add Kailin's own as they emerge}

Each clip on the sheet shows its series + episode ("Day in the Life · Fan Fest episode"). The campaign tracker keeps episode continuity so the feed reads as a story.

## Funnel ratio (every post gets a conversion role)
Default for the 30-day window: **70 build / 20 soft-sell / 10 hard-CTA.**
- **Awareness / build** · grows audience, no ask. Most personal content.
- **Soft-CTA** · plants the event (countdown sticker, "something's coming", link in bio mention).
- **Hard-CTA** · direct ask (ticket link, "comment PLATE for the invite", story countdown).
The Director must name each ready post's role and keep the daily mix roughly on ratio. Personal brand feeds Plated bookings, it does not run parallel to them.

## Campaign tracker (persists across days)
Alongside the daily sheet, maintain a 30-day board:
```
# Plated Circuit · Road to July 30 · campaign tracker
Days to launch: {n}
Series progress:
  - 30 Days to Plated: episodes posted {list} · next beat {what}
  - Founder Diaries: {count} posted · last {date}
CTA cadence: hard-CTAs in last 7 days {n} (target ~1-2/week ramping near the date)
Thin spots: {which pillar/series has gone quiet, where the arc needs a beat}
Milestones: {ticket pushes, reveal moments, final-week countdown}
```
This is what stops the feed resetting every morning and makes it build to the event.
