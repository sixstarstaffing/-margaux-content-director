## Filling rules
- Captions/hooks via `social-content` / `event-promo-content` pointed at PERSONAL-BRAND.md voice corpus, never generic.
- Post order: timeliness first (hot moments lead), then series cadence, then conversion goal. NOT "easiest first".
- Coverage line is mandatory, it is the proof nothing was skimmed.
- Personal page leads volume, Plated leads curation, keep the funnel ratio roughly 70/20/10.
